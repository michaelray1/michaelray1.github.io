I am a recent graduate of the University of Cincinnati (UC) and now a first-year grad student at Stony Brook University (SBU) At SBU, I am working towards an M.A. in physics, after which I will pursue a career in the data science industry.

Before I began college at UC, I had lived my entire life in Columbus, Ohio with my parents and two brothers. Throughout my childhood I had a passion for both academics and athletics, competing in high school sports year round. In my free time now, I especially enjoy playing guitar, snowboarding, reading, and cheering on Ohio sports teams (especially the Cleveland Browns and Ohio State Buckeyes).
